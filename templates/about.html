About Dr. Parasuram Kooderi

Dr. Parasuram Kooderi, BAMS, MD (Ayurveda – Psychiatry) is a highly experienced Ayurvedic physician specializing in sexual health, infertility management, and mental well-being. He is widely respected for his ethical medical practice, patient-centered approach, and commitment to confidentiality.

With extensive clinical experience, including three years of international medical practice in Russia, Dr. Parasuram has successfully managed 200+ infertility cases and numerous sexual and psychological health conditions. He currently serves as the Project Medical Officer at the Government Ayurveda Mental Hospital, Kottakkal.

Through Sperra Advanced Ayurveda, Dr. Parasuram integrates classical Ayurvedic wisdom with modern clinical evaluation to deliver personalized, root-cause–oriented treatment. His approach emphasizes safe, natural, and long-term healing in a respectful, judgment-free environment.

Our Approach

Ethical, Holistic & Root-Cause Focused Ayurvedic Care

At Sperra Advanced Ayurveda, our approach is centered on confidential, patient-focused, and evidence-based Ayurvedic treatment. We focus on identifying the root cause of sexual health concerns, infertility, and mental health conditions rather than offering temporary symptom relief.

What We Do

Holistic Care for Sexual & Mental Well-Being

At Sperra Advanced Ayurveda, we specialize in treating sexual health disorders, male and female infertility, stress-related conditions, anxiety, sleep disturbances, and emotional imbalance through authentic Ayurvedic medicine.
